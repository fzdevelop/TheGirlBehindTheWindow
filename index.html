About the Book

The Girl Behind the Window is an emotional romantic fiction that explores unspoken love, loneliness, and silent connection. Through the eyes of Faraz, the story captures the pain of loving someone from a distance, someone whose name remains unnamed to protect the purity of the bond they shared. A deeply moving tale for readers who believe that the strongest emotions are often the ones left unsaid.
